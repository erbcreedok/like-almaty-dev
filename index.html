Like Almaty

About us

Tour Operator "LikeAlmaty" was founded by an experienced professional team coming from various Tourist Holdings, specialized in the planning, booking and management of any travel operation related to groups in Kazakhstan and worldwide.

We always work thinking of you, of your needs, but above all, of your expectations. We know that every detail is important, and for this reason make our best effort to care for all those "little things" which will always make the difference so that your trip will be exceptional.

Our service is characterized by being flexible and creative in the design of itineraries;
For this we are diligent in the selection of our transport personnel, guides and all those persons involved in the operation.

We intend to show you a variety of nature places, national shows, and even meet with some of the people who live here.

We make sure that your vacation matches and exceeds your interests, and expectations.

Our Services

Individual approach

Our professional and experienced team always at your disposal and ready to arrange every element of your holiday including flights, transfers excursion, tours and any other additional services
